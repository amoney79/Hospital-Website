About Our Pad & Tampon Project

Menstrual poverty affects thousands of girls in our region. Many miss school during their period due to lack of access to sanitary products. Our initiative aims to change this.

Impact

Providing menstrual products to over 500 schoolgirls across the Jacaranda region, reducing school absenteeism.

Mission

Promote menstrual health education and remove stigma surrounding menstruation in our community.

Sustainability

Building long-term solutions through education, awareness, and consistent community support.
