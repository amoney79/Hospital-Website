About Us

We Are Marafiki K.C A Healthcare Provider

Marafiki Community Hospital Jacaranda is a modern, patient-centered facility in Watamu dedicated to providing compassionate and affordable healthcare.

With qualified doctors across specialties like Diabetic, Ultrasound, Hypertension,Inpatient and Outpatient, the hospital combines advanced medical technology with a commitment to community well-being.

Our History

Marafiki K.C. Hospital is located in the Jacaranda area and was inaugurated on July 15, 2024 thanks to the efforts of a group of Italian/Kenyan friends. We started off by supporting various projects in the pandemic such as buying and distributing food, collecting and distributing masks, paid a teacher throughout the lock down who taught school to the children in the area, donated needed school supplies as well as medicine, clothing and shoes.

When the pandemic finally ended, the group of “friends” never stopped being there to support those who in the meantime were slowly trying to “restart” even if among many difficulties.

As health care in Kenya is predominantly private, it is inaccessible to many people who do not have sufficient means to be able to pay for a medical consultation or even less hospitalizations, specialized examinations or interventions.

Then came the idea to create this opportunity for the Jacaranda community, a medical dispensary, which could provide affordable medical care for all.

Since opening, the hospital has focused on outreach, maternal & child health, and community education — and continues expanding services with local partners.
