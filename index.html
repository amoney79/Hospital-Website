Emergency Help & Services

Our Emergency Help Services provides first-contact medical care, diagnosis, and treatment for non-emergency conditions. Patients are evaluated by qualified clinicians and referred to specialists when necessary.

Diabetic Clinic

The Diabetic Clinic provides comprehensive care for patients living with diabetes, focusing on blood sugar control, lifestyle management, and prevention of complications.

Ultrasound Services

Our ultrasound unit provides safe, non-invasive imaging services to assist in medical diagnosis, pregnancy monitoring, and internal organ assessment.

Medical Treatment

Blood pressure screening. Hypertension treatment plans. Medication monitoring. Lifestyle & dietary counselling. Malaria & infectious disease tests. Blood and urine analysis. Diabetes and cholesterol testing. Fast and reliable results.

Hypertension Clinic

The Hypertension Clinic specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and long-term management of high blood pressure to reduce the risk of heart disease and stroke.

Delivery Services

Who we Are

Our Mission:

To provide accessible, patient-centered medical care that improves lives and promotes wellness for all.

Our Vision:

To be a trusted leader in healthcare, recognized for innovation, integrity, and compassionate service.

Our Values:

  • Compassion in every interaction
  • Excellence in medical practice and patient care
  • Integrity and respect for all individuals
  • Innovation to meet evolving health needs
  • Community partnership and outreach

Our team of highly skilled doctors, nurses, and specialists work together to deliver comprehensive services — from emergency care and diagnostics to specialized treatments and preventive health programs.

Rooted in our community, we believe healthcare should be inclusive, affordable, and tailored to the unique needs of every patient.

At Marafiki community Hospital, your health is our priority, and your trust is our greatest reward.

Laboratory

Blood and urine analysis
Malaria & infectious disease tests
Diabetes and cholesterol testing
Fast and reliable results

Diabetic

The Diabetic Clinic provides comprehensive care for patients living with diabetes, focusing on blood sugar control, lifestyle management, and prevention of complications.

Blood sugar monitoring
Diabetes medication management
Nutrition and lifestyle guidance
Complication screening & follow-up

Maternity

Our maternity unit offers safe and compassionate care for mothers before, during, and after childbirth, ensuring the health of both mother and baby.

Antenatal (prenatal) care
Skilled delivery services
Postnatal care & support
Family planning counselling

Blood pressure screening
Hypertension treatment plans

Obstetric (pregnancy) scans
